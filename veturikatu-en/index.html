Check-in instructions

Veturikatu 8 A28, Turku, 20100, Finland

You can pick up the keys from R-kioski convenience store in Turku bus station, 750 meters from the apartment. R-kioski is open Monday to Friday between 6am to 9pm, Satuday between 8am to 9pm Sunday between 9am to 9pm Our place has exceptionally early check-in possibility. You can check in whenever you have picked up the keys.

Step 1:If you didn't reserve a parking place, you can navigate directly to address 'Veturikatu 8 Turku' and enter the building. Take the elevator or stairs to floor number 4. Your apartment is number 28. The door only has a number but no name.

Step 2:If you reserved a parking place, navigate to address 'Vaunukatu 3 Turku'. Use the given remote control to open the garage door and drive in.

Step 3:Park on place 166 on the right side. Then walk as indicated by the arrow.

Step 4:Keep walking.. left... and left.

Step 5:Opposite to parking place number 253 you will find a glass door and another glass door behind it. Enter from both and you will find the elevator. Go to 4th floor. Your apartment is number 28. There is only a number on the door and no name.
